Please upload treated DNA methylation files as Instruction refered.



Your input data will automatically compared to 17 types of tumors. Of note, you will get Normal classification results, if your input data looks more like from a normal tissue than a tumor tissue. And if your input data is not in the reference cohort, final classification results will be presented. However, the diagram will notice you the not-in-cohort possible.
