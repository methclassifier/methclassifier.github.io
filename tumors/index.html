Tumors

The following 17 types of tumors form the reference cohort, to which your input data will automatically compared. Of note, you will get Normal classification results, if your input data looks more like from a normal tissue than a tumor tissue. And if your input data is not in the reference cohort, final classification results will be presented. However, the diagram will notice you the not-in-cohort possible.
